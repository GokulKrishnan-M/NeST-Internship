Kozhikode

City of Spices

Kozhikode, also known as Calicut, is famous for its historic trade links, spice markets, and coastal beauty. It was the landing spot of Vasco da Gama and has a vibrant cultural scene.

Tourist Attractions
  • Kozhikode Beach
  • Mananchira Square
  • Beypore Port
  • Kappad Beach
