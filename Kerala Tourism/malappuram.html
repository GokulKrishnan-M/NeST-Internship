Malappuram

Land of Hills and History

Malappuram is a historically significant and culturally rich district in Kerala. Known for its scenic beauty, mosques, and temples, it also features many rivers and hilly landscapes.

Tourist Attractions
  • Kottakkunnu
  • Teak Museum, Nilambur
  • Adyanpara Waterfalls
  • Thirunavaya Temple
