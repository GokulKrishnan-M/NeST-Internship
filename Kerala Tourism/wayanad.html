Wayanad

Green Paradise of Kerala

Wayanad is a lush and hilly district known for its cool climate, tea and coffee plantations, and tribal heritage. It offers scenic beauty and trekking opportunities in the Western Ghats.

Tourist Attractions
  • Edakkal Caves
  • Banasura Sagar Dam
  • Soochipara Waterfalls
  • Chembra Peak
