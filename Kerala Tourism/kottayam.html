Kottayam

Land of letters, latex, and lakes

Kottayam is known for its rich literary tradition, high literacy rate, and natural beauty. Surrounded by scenic backwaters and lush greenery, it plays an important role in Kerala’s rubber industry. The district is also famous for its many publishing houses and educational institutions.

Tourist Attractions
  • Vembanad Lake
  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary
  • Ilaveezhapoonchira
  • St. Mary’s Church, Bharananganam
