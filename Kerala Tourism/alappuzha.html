Alappuzha

Venice of the East

Alappuzha is famous for its scenic backwaters, houseboat cruises, and lush paddy fields. The annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race attracts thousands of visitors from around the world.

Tourist Attractions
  • Houseboat Cruises
  • Alappuzha Beach
  • Kuttanad
  • Pathiramanal Island
