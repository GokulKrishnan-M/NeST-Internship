Pathanamthitta

Pilgrim Capital of Kerala

Pathanamthitta is known for its religious significance, being the gateway to the famous Sabarimala Temple. The district is blessed with scenic rivers, waterfalls, and lush forest landscapes.

Tourist Attractions
  • Sabarimala Temple
  • Gavi Eco-Tourism
  • Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls
  • Konni Elephant Training Center
