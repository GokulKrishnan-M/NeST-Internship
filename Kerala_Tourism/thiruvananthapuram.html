Thiruvananthapuram

The Capital City of Kerala

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of Kerala, is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful beaches, and historical monuments. It is home to the famous Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple and scenic Kovalam Beach.

Tourist Attractions
  • Kovalam Beach
  • Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple
  • Napier Museum
  • Ponmudi Hill Station
