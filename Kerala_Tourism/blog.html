Travel Tips
  • Best time to visit Kerala: October to March for pleasant weather.
  • Carry light cotton clothes and an umbrella during monsoon.
  • Try local transport like houseboats and ferries for a unique experience.
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly at temples and religious sites.
Featured Destinations
  • Munnar: Famous for tea gardens, cool climate, and scenic hills.
  • Alappuzha: Enjoy a houseboat cruise through the tranquil backwaters.
  • Kovalam: Relax on golden beaches and try water sports.
  • Wayanad: Explore wildlife, waterfalls, and spice plantations.
Festivals of Kerala

Kerala is renowned for its vibrant festivals that reflect the state's rich culture and traditions:

  • Onam: The grand harvest festival celebrated with floral carpets, boat races, and the traditional Onam Sadya feast.
  • Vishu: Marks the Malayalam New Year with rituals and fireworks.
  • Thrissur Pooram: Famous for its spectacular elephant procession and fireworks display.
  • Theyam: A ritual art form celebrated in the temples of North Kerala.
