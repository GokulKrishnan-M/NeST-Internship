Kannur

Land of Looms and Lores

Kannur is known for its traditional theyyam performances, pristine beaches, and handloom industries. It also has colonial forts and cultural landmarks reflecting its rich past.

Tourist Attractions
  • St. Angelo Fort
  • Payyambalam Beach
  • Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach
  • Parassinikadavu Temple
